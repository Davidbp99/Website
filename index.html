Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)



What is your process? (how I work)

Prep: Start of with getting a better picture of your project and asking questions. I as well look at the workload and advice on services (optimization, map protection and so on) that you self can think about. Then we agree on a price
Production: In this stage I work on the project. I will as well go back and forth with the work I do to make sure you get what you want.
Delivery: When you got everything you want (unless we have hit the map limit and can't work around it). I will then send the invoice via stripe and when its paid you will have the vmf and bsp(with the content in it) sent via discord, gmodstore or email.



I got new ideas to the project. Will the price change if I ask to have it done?

Depends. If you come just 24 hours after we agreed it won't be raised by a lot (depends what you want me to add into the plans). But if you come 3, 4 days later or near/on dead line date and got ideas as (not limited to) 1. adding new areas. 2. Rexture the map. 3.Remake A,B,C house



I only want to pay X $ for A,B,C Work

I'm always happy to work on fitting the service your budget. To lower anyones map price total is that you would take away A, B or just C work. For example if you know how to make textures for maps (so it works for hammer editor). Then that work is not needed to be done by the me. Means the total is lowered.



Can I get the map for cheaper?

I'm happy to remove services that you or I have put forward from the price total. As well make the design more simple. A cheaper product (map) can mean its worse in one or many ways. (not in all situations. As new mappers starts of selling for cheap or even free). It can be from skipping optimization (means players will lag). Map limits optimization can mean that next mapper you hire will need to charge you for doing map optimization.



What is optimization and map limits optimization? Is it not the same thing?

For me when I say optimization I normally mean fps. When I say map limits optimization I mean the limits the map is on. If a map goes above just one limit it won't render fully. It will throw out error and trying to load into the map will be broken or not even there.



Can I get the map files / Can you upload the map to a test server before I pay?

No. I'm happy to screenshare the map on discord. You will have the files after payment is done via stripe



Can we split up the invoice to friend A, B and me

I'm not always fine splitting the invoice, because of the fees for each invoice. If we can't resolve it with just one paying, we can split it up.



Can I download x map and edit it for my server?

All vmf you can find at my github you can edit. Just want some credits in the workshop upload or on the map (or both). Any clients map is up to them to decide what they do with the vmf and map
