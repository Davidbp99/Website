SCP Map

First ever bigger map from the ground up

Story Behind the map

I started making this map sometime back on Mar 14 (2021). I started this project as I felt there were no maps that really had what I wanted. Like gamemasters areas, sim rooms, some SCPs that were not made as they should (according to wiki), Outside area not having a big enemy base that had sim rooms/debrief/bunks, and then could support custom places.
